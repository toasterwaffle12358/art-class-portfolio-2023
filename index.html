3D photography process
In making theme project 1, I went through many production steps. I summarized the main ones here. Not seen here is the photogrammetry process of taking a bunch of photos of a location or object and importing them into an app that will estimate the depth and geometry of the space based on your photos.
pixelsorting
Throught this class I learned many new techniques for editing and processing photos. One such technique I learned is called pixelsorting, I used this to make the image on the left. I also got more familiar with the program "darktable". The skills and software I have learned from this class will help me outside of photography in post processing my blender renders.

GROWTH REFLECTION
I chose high speed rail because public transit and non car based infrastructure really interest me. Even outside of climate change, I think investing more resources in our public transit system is important. At a large level individual carbon footprint is not as important as it is made out to be, but changing how you commute to work or school every day for every person could have massive positive affects on global climate change. I also think with so many other countries using high speed rail already, we know it's something that is tested and works.
